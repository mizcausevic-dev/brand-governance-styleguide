Proof rail

Use for metrics, short outcome summaries, and operator posture snapshots under the hero or above a launch table.

  • Metrics should fit the shared card and label grammar.
  • Use the status color lane intentionally, not decoratively.
  • Proof text should remain short enough to scan in one pass.
